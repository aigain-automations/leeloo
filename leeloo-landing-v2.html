Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Leeloo different from ChatGPT or other AI assistants? ▼
Unlike ChatGPT, which only suggests actions, Leeloo actually executes them. It connects directly to your mailbox, calendar, and contacts to send emails, schedule meetings, update contacts, and much more. You speak — Leeloo does.
Is my data safe and private? ▼
Absolutely. Your data stays yours. Leeloo never shares or trains on your content. All operations comply with GDPR, APPI, and CCPA regulations. We use industry-standard encryption and secure OAuth connections to your accounts.
How long does setup take? ▼
Setup takes about 15 minutes. Simply connect your Google or Outlook account, tell Leeloo who your frequent contacts are (with their nicknames), set your preferred language and location — and you're ready to go. Business and Expert plans include onboarding sessions to help you get the most out of Leeloo, but you can also set it up yourself easily with our detailed setup guide.
What languages does Leeloo support? ▼
Leeloo supports 30+ languages, including English, French, German, Portuguese, Spanish, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and more. You can communicate in your preferred language, and Leeloo will respond accordingly — even composing emails in languages you don't speak.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes, all plans can be cancelled at any time. The Starter plan has no commitment and includes a 14-day free trial. Business and Expert plans have a 2-month minimum.
Which plan should I choose? ▼
Choose Starter if you need basic email, calendar, and contact management. Choose Business if you want long-term memory, document processing, and 3 additional modules. Go with Expert if you need voice activation (similar to Siri or Alexa), unlimited messages, and 6 modules.
Should I install Leeloo from Google Play or the App Store? ▼
No installation needed. Leeloo is a fully web-based application that works directly from your browser — on both smartphone and computer.
Can Leeloo integrate with other software I use? ▼
Yes, Leeloo integrates with many leading tools such as Outlook, Gmail, Google Calendar, Notion, Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and more.
Do I need technical skills to use Leeloo? ▼
Absolutely not! Leeloo was designed to be accessible to everyone — no code, no complex configuration. You simply interact with your AI assistant through voice or text, on an intuitive interface similar to WhatsApp, Messenger, Line, WeChat, or Telegram.
